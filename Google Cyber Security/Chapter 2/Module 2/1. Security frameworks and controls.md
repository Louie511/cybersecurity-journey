## This lecture emphasizes the critical role of a security analyst in protecting both organizations and individuals from the impact of data breaches.

## The Importance of a Security Analyst's Role

- Security analysts do more than just protect organizations; they safeguard individuals' financial stability and reputations from data breaches.
- Daily work involves applying security frameworks, controls, and design principles to protect people and organizations.

## Security Frameworks and Controls

- The course will delve into security frameworks, controls, and design principles, and their application in security audits.
- The NIST Cybersecurity Framework is highlighted as a key tool for ensuring the protection and compliance of customer tools and personal work devices through security controls.
