## 🔷 This module will introduce playbooks as a crucial tool for security professionals in responding to threats and vulnerabilities.

## 🔺 Introduction to Playbooks

- Playbooks help security teams address threats, risks, or vulnerabilities identified by SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) tools.
- They serve as a guide for incident response, ensuring a structured and effective approach.

## 🔺 Incident Response Phases

- The video will delve into the six phases of incident response.
- Understanding these phases is essential for managing security incidents systematically.
