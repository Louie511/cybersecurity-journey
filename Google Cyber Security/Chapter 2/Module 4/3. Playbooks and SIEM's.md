## 🔷 This lecture explains how Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) tools and incident response playbooks are used together to manage and reduce organizational threats.

## 🔺 Incident Response Playbooks

- Playbooks are guides that help security professionals quickly and accurately mitigate security issues.
- They provide structure, ensure compliance, and outline communication and documentation processes.

## 🔺 Using a Playbook for a SIEM Alert

- When a SIEM alerts to a potential threat, a playbook guides the analyst through the necessary steps, starting with assessing the alert's validity.
- The playbook then outlines actions and tools to contain the threat, such as isolating an infected system to prevent malware spread.

## 🔺 Post-Incident Activities and Playbook Evolution

- After containing and eliminating the threat, the playbook instructs on post-incident activities like creating reports and coordinating with security teams.
- Playbooks are dynamic documents that are frequently updated and improved based on new threats and lessons learned from past security incidents.
