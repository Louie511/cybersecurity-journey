## 🔷 This lecture explains how playbooks are used by security teams to address security-related issues and respond to incidents.

## 🔺 Playbooks and SIEM Tools

- Playbooks provide a consistent list of actions for security teams to follow during an incident, ensuring a standardized response.
- They are often used with Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) tools, which flag unusual behavior, prompting analysts to use playbooks for resolution.

## 🔺 Playbooks and SOAR Tools

- Playbooks are also integrated with Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response (SOAR) tools, which automate repetitive tasks.
- SOAR tools can automatically block suspicious activity, and then analysts refer to playbooks for further steps to resolve the issue.

## 🔺 Key Takeaways

- Playbooks, also known as runbooks, offer detailed instructions for security teams to follow in the event of an incident.
- By clearly defining roles and actions, playbooks help minimize the impact of security incidents and protect an organization's critical assets.
