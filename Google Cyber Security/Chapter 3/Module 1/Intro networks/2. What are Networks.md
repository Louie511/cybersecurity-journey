## 🔷 This lecture explains the fundamental concepts of computer networks, including their definition, how devices communicate, and the different types of networks.

## 🔺 What is a Network?

- A network is a group of connected devices that can communicate with each other.
- Examples include laptops, cell phones, smart devices in a home, or workstations, printers, and servers in an office.

## 🔺 Device Communication and Identification

- Devices on a network communicate using network cables or wireless connections.
- Each device uses unique identifiers, such as IP and MAC addresses, to locate each other and ensure proper communication.

## 🔺 Types of Networks

### Local Area Network (LAN): 
- Spans a small geographical area like a home, office, or school. For instance, your phone connecting to your home Wi-Fi forms a LAN.

### Wide Area Network (WAN): 
- Covers a large geographical area, such as a city, state, or country. The internet is a prime example of a WAN, allowing communication across vast distances.
