## 🔷 This lecture explains the critical need for secure networks by outlining common attack methods and their potential consequences.

## 🔺 Why Secure Networks Are Essential

- Networks face constant threats from malicious hackers who use methods like malware, spoofing, and packet sniffing.
- Attacks can disrupt operations through actions such as packet flooding, leading to severe organizational damage.

## 🔺 Impact of Network Attacks

- Cyberattacks can result in the leakage of valuable or confidential information, harming an organization's reputation.
- Mitigating these attacks often incurs significant financial costs and demands considerable time from organizations.

## 🔺 Real-World Example of a Cyberattack

- The 2014 Home Depot breach serves as a notable example, where hackers compromised servers with malware.
- This attack led to the theft of credit and debit card information from over 56 million customers, highlighting the extensive damage cyberattacks can inflict.
