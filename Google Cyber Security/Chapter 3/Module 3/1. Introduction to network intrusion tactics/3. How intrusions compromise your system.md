## 🔷 This lecure focuses on understanding network vulnerabilities, common attack methods like interception and backdoor attacks, and their potential impacts on organizations.

## 🔺 Network Attack Motivations and Vulnerabilities

- Attackers have diverse motivations, including financial gain, personal reasons, political agendas, or being disgruntled employees.
- Security analysts must remain vigilant for vulnerabilities and act quickly to mitigate them.

## 🔺 Types of Network Attacks

### ⚫ Network Interception Attacks: 
- These involve capturing and inspecting data in transit (packet sniffing) to steal information or alter transmissions, potentially causing damage or interrupting operations.

### ⚫ Backdoor Attacks: 
- These exploit intentional weaknesses left by programmers or administrators, or are installed by attackers, to bypass normal security and gain persistent access, leading to malware installation, DoS attacks, or data theft.

## 🔺 Potential Impacts of Network Attacks

### ⚫ Financial: 
- Attacks can lead to significant revenue loss from interrupted operations, high reparation costs, and potential litigation expenses if customer data is compromised.

### ⚫ Reputation and Public Safety: 
- Cyberattacks can damage an organization's reputation, leading to loss of trust, and in cases involving government networks or critical infrastructure, can pose a threat to public safety.
