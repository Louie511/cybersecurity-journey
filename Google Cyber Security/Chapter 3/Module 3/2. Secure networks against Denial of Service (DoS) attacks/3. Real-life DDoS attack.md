## 🔷 This lecture focuses on a significant 2016 Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack that targeted DNS servers, causing widespread outages for numerous organizations.

## 🔺 Understanding DDoS Attacks

- Volumetric DDoS attacks overwhelm networks with excessive unwanted data, making servers unable to serve legitimate users.
- Such attacks can lead to financial losses, inability to meet customer needs, and damage an organization's reputation.

## 🔺 The 2016 DNS Server Attack

- A botnet, created by university students and later made publicly available, was used to launch the attack.
- The botnet sent tens of millions of DNS requests to a major DNS service provider, causing its systems to shut down and making websites inaccessible.

## 🔺 Key Takeaways for Security Analysts

- DDoS attacks are serious and can be very damaging to organizations.
- Security analysts should be aware of mitigation strategies to protect networks, such as distributing important operations across dynamically scalable hosts.
